Computational Models for Prognosis of Medication for Cardiovascular Diseases

Prasad, V.K., Rishabh, R., Shenoy, V., Meleet, M., Cholli, N.G. (2023). In: Das, S., Saha, S., Coello Coello, C.A., Bansal, J.C. (eds) Advances in Data-Driven Computing and Intelligent Systems. ADCIS 2022. Lecture Notes in Networks and Systems, vol 698. Springer, Singapore. https://doi.org/10.1007/978-981-99-3250-4_24

  • Research Focus: Clinical Decision Support System using deep learning to predict cardiovascular medications from 117 biological markers.
  • Key Results: Achieved 80–96% accuracy in predicting 15 heart medications using custom neural networks.
  • Impact: Addresses a global healthcare challenge—cardiovascular diseases cause 32% of worldwide deaths.

    Customized Name Entity Recognition for Medical Data using BERT

    Vinayak Krishan Prasad , Vikram Shenoy , Merin Meleet, (2022). In: INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF ENGINEERING RESEARCH & TECHNOLOGY (IJERT) Volume 11, Issue 07 (July 2022), ISN: 2278-0181. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18412636

  • Research Focus: Fine-tuned BERT model for structured information extraction from unstructured Electronic Health Records (EHRs).
  • Key Results: Achieved 99% accuracy in medical entity recognition.
  • Impact: Enables doctors, researchers, and patients to extract valuable insights from clinical notes automatically.
